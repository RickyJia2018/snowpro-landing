Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 5, 2026

Welcome to SnowPro. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application and related web services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide and improve our services, including:

  • Account Information: When you register, we collect your email address, username, and secure passwords.
  • Skiing/Snowboarding Videos: If you use our remote coaching features, we upload skiing or snowboarding videos you record to our cloud storage so verified coaches can analyze them and provide feedback.
  • Device Tokens & Notification Data: We collect device notification tokens (FCM and JPush) to deliver critical notifications (e.g., chat notifications, coaching feedback notifications).
  • Payment & Transaction Info: When you purchase tokens, transaction details are processed and validated through third-party platforms (Apple App Store / Google Play Store). We do not directly store your credit card information.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data for the following purposes:

  • To provide, operate, and maintain our remote coaching and video analysis services.
  • To manage user accounts and authenticate user sessions.
  • To process payments and verify token purchase transactions.
  • To send push notifications regarding system updates, message receipts, or course updates.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data. We only share information in the following circumstances:

  • With Verification Partners: Purchase receipts are verified via Apple and Google APIs.
  • For Service Operation: Coaching videos are shared with the specific certified coach you select for review.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law, we may disclose your information to comply with legal processes or safety requirements.

4. Data Deletion and Retention

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary to provide the services. You have the right to request the deletion of your account and all associated personal data (including uploaded videos and profile information) by contacting us at our support email.

5. Children's Privacy

Our services are not target-marketed to children under the age of 13. If we learn we have collected personal data of a child under 13 without appropriate parental consent, we will take steps to delete that data as quickly as possible.
